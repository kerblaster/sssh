Ruleset Source

Required Settings
  • Mode: Stock
  • Stocks: 4
  • Time Limit: 8
  • Handicap: Off
  • Input Buffer: Off
  • Team Attack: On
  • Damage Ratio: 100%
  • Items: Off
  • Pause: Off
  • All characters available

Match Procedure
  1. Select your characters. May elect to double blind (secretly tell third party)
  2. RPS to determine port priority
  3. Stage strike to determine first stage (Singles: 1-2-2-1, Doubles: 1-2-2-1). Player with port priority gets first ban.
  4. Start first game
  5. Winner bans 3 stages from both neutral and counterpicks
  6. Loser picks a stage from the list. May not pick any stage previously won on unless mutually agreed to (DSR)
  7. Winner may change character
  8. Loser may change character
  9. Start Game
  10. Repeat steps 5-9 until set is won/lost
Misc
  • Ties: Stocks->Percentage->Replay (w/ 1 stock and 3 min)
  • Giga Bowser is banned
