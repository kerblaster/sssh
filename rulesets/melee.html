23 Super Smash Set Helper
Super Smash Set Helper
Melee Ruleset
Melee Ruleset
Basic Information
  • Original Release: November 21, 2001 (JAP)
  • Original Platform: Nintendo Gamecube
Ruleset Source
  • From The Apex 2015 Rulebook
Required Settings
  • Mode: Stock
  • Stocks: 4
  • Time Limit: 8
  • Handicap: Off
  • Team Attack: On
  • Damage Ratio: 100%
  • Items: Off
  • Pause: Off
  • All characters available
Stages
Neutral Starters:
  • Battlefield
  • Dreamland
  • Final Destination
  • Yoshi's Story
  • Fountain of Dreams (singles only)
  • Pokemon Stadium (doubles only)

Counterpicks:
  • Pokemon Stadium
  • Kongo Jungle (doubles only)
Match Procedure
  1. Select your characters. May elect to double blind (secretly tell third party)
  2. RPS to determine port priority
  3. Stage strike to determine first stage (Singles: 1-2-1, Doubles: 1-1). Player with port priority gets second ban.
  4. Start first game
  5. Winner bans a stage from both neutral and counterpicks
  6. Loser picks a stage from the list. May not pick any stage previously won on unless mutually agreed to (DSR)
  7. Winner may change character
  8. Loser may change character
  9. Start Game
  10. Repeat steps 5-9 until set is won/lost
Misc
  • Ties: Stocks->Percentage->Replay
  • Master Hand is banned
  • Glitches are banned (ex. Freeze Glitch, Soul Stunner)
  • Stalling with moves is banned (ex. Rising Pound, Wall Bomber)
