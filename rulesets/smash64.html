Super Smash Set Helper
Smash 64 Ruleset
Smash 64 Ruleset
Basic Information
  • Original Release: January 21, 1999 (JAP)
  • Original Platform: Nintendo 64
Ruleset Source
  • From The Apex 2015 Rulebook
Required Settings
  • Mode: Stock
  • Stocks: 5
  • Handicap: Off
  • Team Attack: On
  • Damage Ratio: 100%
  • Items: Off
  • Pause: Off
  • All characters available
Stages
Starters:
  • Dreamland
  • Hyrule Castle (doubles only)
  • Congo Jungle (doubles only)

Counterpicks:
  • Peach's Castle
  • Congo Jungle
Match Procedure
  1. Select your characters. May elect to double blind (secretly tell third party)
  2. RPS to determine port priority
  3. Stage strike to determine first stage (Singles: automatically Dreamland, Doubles: 1-1). Player with port priority gets second ban.
  4. Start first game
  5. Winner bans a stage from both neutral and counterpicks.
  6. Loser picks a stage from the list. May not pick any counterpick stage previously won on unless mutually agreed to (DSR)
  7. Winner may change character
  8. Loser may change character
  9. Start Game
  10. Repeat steps 5-9 until set is won/lost
Misc
  • Stalling is not allowed
  • Any stage allowed if mutually agreed to
