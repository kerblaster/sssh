Brawl Ruleset
Brawl Ruleset
Basic Information
  • Original Release: January 31, 2008 (JAP)
  • Original Platform: Wii
Ruleset Source
  • From The Apex 2015 Rulebook
Required Settings
  • Mode: Stock
  • Stocks: 3
  • Time Limit: 8
  • Handicap: Off
  • Team Attack: On
  • Damage Ratio: 100%
  • Items: Off
  • Pause: Off
  • All characters available
Stages
Neutral Starters:
  • Battlefield
  • Final Destination
  • Lylat Cruise
  • Smashville
  • Yoshi's Island

Counterpicks:
  • Castle Siege
  • Delfino Plaza*
  • Halberd*
  • Pokemon Stadium

*Note: Meta Knight is not legal on Halberd, and Delfino Plaza
Match Procedure
  1. Select your characters. May elect to double blind (secretly tell third party)
  2. RPS to determine port priority
  3. Stage strike to determine first stage (Singles: 1-2-1, Doubles: 1-2-1). Player with port priority gets second ban.
  4. Start first game
  5. Winner bans 2 stages from both neutral and counterpicks
  6. Loser picks a stage from the list. May not pick any stage previously won on unless mutually agreed to (DSR)
  7. Winner may change character
  8. Loser may change character
  9. Start Game
  10. Repeat steps 5-9 until set is won/lost
Misc
  • Ties: Stocks->LGL of 15->Percentage->Replay
  • Meta Knight is not legal on Halberd, and Delfino Plaza
  • Double Meta Knight is not allowed in Doubles
  • Meta Knight's Infinite Dimensional Cape Tactic is banned
  • 35 Ledge Grab Limit (LGL). If one player exceeds limit, that player auto loses.
  • Scrooging is banned: travelling ender the stage from ledge more than once. Not count if someone takes damage.
  • Stalling with moves is banned (ex. Invisible, Infinite/Chain grab past 300%, unreachable)
  • Initiator of suicide move wins if game ends in a suicidal finish.
