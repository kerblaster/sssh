Super Smash Set Helper is a web-based mobile app that walks players through an entire competitive (BO1, BO3, BO5) set obeying the legal rules of a chosen smash title.

Supported smash titles include Super Smash Bros, Super Smash Bros Melee, Super Smash Bros Brawl, Project M, and Super Smash Bros for Wii-U/DS.
